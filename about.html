How FurCart began

FurCart was founded with a simple frustration: why was it so hard to find pet gear in India that was both high-quality AND affordable?

Our founder Ajay Mishra noticed that most pet stores either stocked cheap, unsafe products — or imported luxury brands with absurd markups. There was nothing in between.

So he built FurCart — a store that brings premium, vet-approved pet gear directly to Indian pet owners at honest prices, delivered across all of India.
