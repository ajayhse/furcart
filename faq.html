❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FurCart

📦 Orders & Shipping
How long does delivery take? ▾
Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days across India. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available for select pin codes. You will receive a tracking link via email once your order is dispatched.
Is shipping free? ▾
Yes! We offer free shipping on all orders above ₹3,299. For orders below that, a flat shipping fee of ₹79 applies.
Do you ship across India? ▾
Yes, we ship to all major cities and most pin codes across India including Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Pune, Kolkata, and more.
How do I track my order? ▾
Visit our Track Order page, enter your Order ID and email address. You can also click the tracking link in your order confirmation email.
💳 Payments
What payment methods do you accept? ▾
We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, BHIM), Razorpay, Paytm, Net Banking, Debit/Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), and Cash on Delivery (COD).
Is Cash on Delivery available? ▾
Yes! COD is available for most pin codes in India. Simply select "Cash on Delivery" at checkout. Please keep exact change ready at the time of delivery.
Is my payment information safe? ▾
Absolutely. All transactions are 256-bit SSL encrypted and processed through Razorpay, which is PCI DSS Level 1 compliant — the highest security standard for payment processing.
↩️ Returns & Refunds
What is your return policy? ▾
We offer hassle-free returns within 30 days of delivery. The item must be unused, unwashed, and in its original packaging. Visit our Returns page to initiate a return.
How long does a refund take? ▾
Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are processed within 5–7 business days back to your original payment method (UPI/Card/Paytm). COD refunds are sent via bank transfer.
What if I received a damaged product? ▾
We're sorry to hear that! Please email us at contact@furcart.com with your order ID and a photo of the damaged item within 48 hours of delivery. We will send a replacement immediately at no extra cost.
🐾 Products
Are your products safe for pets? ▾
Yes! All FurCart products are made from vet-approved, non-toxic, hypoallergenic materials. Our collars and harnesses are tested for safety, durability, and comfort before listing.
How do I choose the right size? ▾
Check our Size Guide for detailed measurements. Generally, measure your pet's neck with a tape and add 2 fingers of space for comfort.
Can I personalise a collar with my pet's name? ▾
Yes! Many of our collars and ID tags support personalisation. Select the personalisation option on the product page and enter your pet's name and your phone number.
