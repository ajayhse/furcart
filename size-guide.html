📐 How to Measure

Use a soft measuring tape around the base of your dog's neck. Add 2 finger widths (~3 cm) for comfortable fit. If between sizes, size up.

🔒 Breakaway Safety Tip

All our cat collars feature a breakaway buckle. The collar should be snug enough to fit 2 fingers underneath. Never too tight!
